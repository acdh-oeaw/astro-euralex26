The registration for the conference will be available soon.

Several registration options, as well as the submission of abstracts, are handled via [Easychair](https://easychair.org/conferences?conf=euralex2026). For further details, please refer to the [Call for Papers](/call-for-papers).

## Fees

| **Registration Fees**                            | **Early Bird (before 10 July)**                                       | **Late (after 10 July)**                                              |
| ------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Regular on-site participant (non-member)\*       | 390 EUR                                                               | 520 EUR                                                               |
| Regular on-site participant (EURALEX member)\*\* | 350 EUR                                                               | 480 EUR                                                               |
| Student on-site participant\*\*\*                | 280 EUR                                                               | 380 EUR                                                               |
| Virtual participant (non presenter)\*\*\*        | 60 EUR                                                                | 110 EUR                                                               |
| Conference dinner                                | 35 EUR                                                                | 35 EUR                                                                |
| Social programme                                 | [click here for details](https://euralex2026.at/en/social-programme/) | [click here for details](https://euralex2026.at/en/social-programme/) |

\* This fee includes EURALEX membership until the end of the next congress. Please note that this membership category does not include a subscription to IJL or the OUP benefits.

\*\* The reduced rate for EURALEX members is available to participants who have paid their membership fees by 31 January 2026 via the OUP website.

\*\*\* This fee does not include EURALEX membership. For student participants, proof of student status will be required.
