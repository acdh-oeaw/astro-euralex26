**The registration for the XXII EURALEX International Congress is now open:**

<LinkButton label="Register here" item={{"discriminant":"external","value":"https://indico.global/e/euralex2026"}} />

Each submission must include at least one author who will present on-site and who has paid the registration fee.

To qualify for the early-bird rate, the registration must be completed by **17 July 2026**. The registration fee includes full lunch on the three congress days, a welcome reception, and all coffee breaks.

Payment is processed through the registration system; please follow the steps provided. If you require an invoice issued **in the name of your institution only** (without your personal name), please contact us at [euralex2026@oeaw.ac.at](mailto:euralex2026@oeaw.ac.at) with your details (institution, address, VAT number of the institution, and the fee category you wish to pay – see below).

## Workshops

Four complimentary workshops are available to all registered, fee-paying participants: [https://euralex2026.at/en/workshops/](https://euralex2026.at/en/workshops/)

## Cancellations & Refund Policy

Cancellations received before **1 August 2026** will be refunded in full, minus a processing fee of € 30.

Cancellations received between **1 August 2026** and **1 September 2026** will be subject to a 50% cancellation fee.

NO REFUNDS will be made for cancellations received after **1 September 2026**.

All cancellations must be submitted in writing to [euralex2026@oeaw.ac.at](mailto:euralex2026@oeaw.ac.at).
