The venue is located in Vienna's historic city center (1st district) and is easily accessible by public transportation.

---

**Getting from Vienna Airport (VIE)**\
By Public Transport (recommended)

**Option 1 - S7 suburban train / ICE /RJX**

* Take the S7 train / ICE or RJX to **Wien Hauptbahnhof**
* Change to subway line U1 towards *Leopoldau*
* Exit at **Wien Stephansplatz**
* The venue is about an 5-minute walk away

Travel time: approx. 35 minutes

**Option 2 - City Airport Train (CAT)**\
\
**During the main line closure in September/October 2026, <Link item={{"discriminant":"pages"}}>[replacement buses](https://www.wien.info/en/travel-info/arrival-departure/airport-to-center/important-information-for-airport-travellers-from-september-2026-1118558)</Link> will also run between Vienna Airport and Wien Mitte - Landstraße every 15 minutes.** **Daily from 06:10 to 23:37.**

Travel time: approx. 30 minutes

---

**Getting from Vienna Central Station (Wien Hauptbahnhof)**

* Take subway line U1 towards *Leopoldau*

- Exit at **Stephansplatz**
- Walk approximately 5 (55 m) minutes to the venue

Alternative route:

* U1 to **Karlsplatz**
* Change to U4 towards *Heiligenstadt*
* Exit at **Schwedenplatz**

Travel time: approx. 15-20 minutes
