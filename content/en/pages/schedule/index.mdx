The EURALEX 2026 Congress **Lexicography in the Age of AI** foresees a wealth of highly interesting programme items, grouped in thematic parallel sessions. Additionally, next to the regular conference programme, a social programme will enable the conference participants to enjoy and get to see (at least bits of) Vienna.&#x20;
