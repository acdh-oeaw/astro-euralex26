## **Instructions for authors**

All accepted extended abstracts will be included in the Book of Abstracts, while accepted papers will be included in the congress proceedings. Papers that have not met the acceptance criteria in the review process can still be presented at the congress, and their extended abstracts will be published in the Book of Abstracts.

When preparing the final version of your paper or extended abstract, please review the comments and instructions from the reviewers meticulously, and follow these instructions.
