## EURALEX 2026 Grants and Financial Support

Thanks to the support of EURALEX and the A. S. Hornby Educational Trust, EURALEX 2026 is able to offer a limited number of grants to facilitate participation in the congress. These grants are intended to reduce financial barriers and to support broader participation, especially for students, early-career researchers, and delegates with limited institutional funding or from low-income countries.

### Tanneke Schoonheim Grants 

Up to eight participants will receive a grant covering the full conference registration fee (Early Bird). 

### A. S. Hornby Trust Grants 

Up to eight participants will receive a grant covering the full conference registration fee (Early Bird). 

### Application Procedure 

Applicants interested in one of the grant schemes are asked to submit their application by email as one single PDF file. 

The application should include: 

* a short motivation letter 
* a short academic CV 
* proof of student or institutional status, where applicable (with stamp & signature of 2026)

Applications should be sent to [euralex2026@oeaw.ac.at ](mailto:euralex2026@oeaw.ac.at)with the relevant subject line. 

### Selection Criteria 

Applications will be assessed on the basis of the following criteria: 

* country of origin
* presenter at EURALEX 2026
* EURALEX membership

### Important Dates 

* Application deadline: 15 June 2026 
* Notification of results: 3 July 2026



Please note: In case you are not awarded a grant you will still have time to profit from the Early Bird registration fee (ends on 10 July 2026).
